How to use MediGuide AI

MediGuide AI helps you understand health information in simple language. Here’s how to use each feature:

Check symptoms

Describe how you feel (e.g. headache, fever, tiredness). You’ll get possible causes and when to see a doctor. You can send follow-up messages in the same chat.

Explain blood test

Upload a PDF of your lab report. The AI will explain results in plain language and point out values that may need attention. Always discuss results with your doctor.

Analyze image

Upload an X-ray or other medical image. You’ll get a simple description of what’s visible. This is not a diagnosis—a radiologist or doctor should review the image.

Medication safety

Enter one or more medication names to see what they’re for, common side effects, and possible interactions. Use this only as a guide; your pharmacist or doctor has the final say.

MediGuide AI is for general information only. It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider.
